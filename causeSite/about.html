The right for children to grow up healthy and happy...


Do you believe that all children, no matter where they are, have the right to grow up healthy and happy?

In rural China, 4 out of every 10 children are left behind by parents who leave to work in urban areas. It affects their education, development and future. Together, we can mitigate the negative impact of migration on children and ensure better protection of their rights.

War, conflict and natural disasters...

As a result of war, conflict and natural disasters, more and more children are displaced and are caught in the dangers of severe food shortages, violence, disease, abuse and loss of educational opportunities.

The dream of the four brothers and sisters of Ajiji...

The Ajiji family lived in a poor mountain village in Liangshan Prefecture, Sichuan. The four brothers and sisters can only live with their elderly grandfather. Fortunately, they have village child welfare director Hailai Day to help and support them.

The happiest moment for the Ajiji brothers and sisters was when they were playing together with the child welfare director Hailai Ri in the village "Children's Home". With the help of Hailai Day, Ajiji's four brothers and sisters can grow up in a stable and happy environment.
